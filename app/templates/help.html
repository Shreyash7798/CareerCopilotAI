How to use CareerCopilot

Everything runs in your browser — no GitHub, code, or technical setup needed.

Getting started (5 steps)

  1. Upload your CV on Profile — PDF or DOCX. This powers match scoring.
  2. Pick companies on Companies — toggle on the firms you care about, or use Get results now on the dashboard for a starter pack.
  3. Review matches on Jobs — filter by score, location, or high priority (70+).
  4. Track applications on Tracker — log status, follow-up dates, and notes.
  5. Turn on alerts in Settings — Telegram chat ID and/or email for instant high-priority job alerts.

How discovery works

The system checks company career pages automatically in the background.

  • Each company you enable is checked about 3 times per day.
  • Fewer companies → checks run more often. More companies → slightly longer between runs.
  • Your dashboard shows the schedule for your company list, not other users.
  • Use Run discovery (top right) for an immediate refresh — once every 15 minutes max.

Your data stays private

  • You only see your own jobs, applications, CV, and Excel export.
  • Other accounts on this portal cannot access your profile or tracker.
  • Sign out when using a shared device.

Common questions

Can I upload a PDF resume?
Yes. PDF and DOCX both work for scoring. DOCX is best if you want tailored resume downloads per job.
Why don’t I see jobs from a company I enabled?
Discovery may take a few minutes after enabling. Some portals block cloud servers — try again later or pick alternative companies.
How do Telegram alerts work?
Ask your admin for the shared bot name. Message the bot, then paste your chat ID in Settings → Notifications → Send test.
